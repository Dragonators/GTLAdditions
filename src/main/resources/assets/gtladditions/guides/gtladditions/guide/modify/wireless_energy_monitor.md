# Wireless Energy Display and Monitoring

<Column gap="15" fullWidth={true}>

<BlockImage id="gtmthings:wireless_energy_monitor" scale="6" />

<Column gap="2" fullWidth={true}>

* <ItemLink id="gtmthings:wireless_energy_monitor" /> displays wireless energy totals with formatting suited for extremely large values.
* Existing average input, average output, fill or drain time, and team or all statistics switching are preserved.
* The machine statistics list formats per-tick energy changes for extremely large values and displays very high voltage tiers correctly.
* Machine entries can still be hovered to view dimension, position, and owner.
